About Us

Our mission is to help companies make a real difference in the fight against climate change.

Companies have an essential role to play, both in reducing their emissions and in funding and building the economy of the future. We're completely rebuilding the infrastructure of the 20th Century, and the process we're reinventing how our cities, economies, and societies will function for the century to come. These changes are happening as we try to mitigate climate change by switching to clean energy and as we adapt to the change that's already here, baked in.

SendGood is about taking control of that change, and shaping it into a future that we want, not sitting back and letting it happen. As we build and adapt, we're creating new inventions that don't just mitigate climate change, but actually improve everyday life. There's not silver bullet for climate change, just a million things to do. And that means that everything, and everyone, can contribute.
